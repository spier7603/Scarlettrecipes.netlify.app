##### The ingredients needed to make Grape Jam:

1. Take 1 1/2 bunches Grapes
1. Take 300 grams Granulated sugar
1. Take 2 tsp Lemon juice




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Grape Jam:

1. Wash the grapes one by one, cut in half, then remove the seeds.
1. Add the Step 1 grapes into a pot, add the granulated sugar and lemon juice on top, then turn on the heat.
1. Remove the grape skin (about 1/3 of the mixture) halfway through cooking. It&#39;s easy to scoop out the skin when it floats to the surface.
1. Simmer for about 30 minutes while adjusting the heat between low and medium, then it&#39;s done!
1. Pour into bottles.
1. Stick a label, and wrap it.
